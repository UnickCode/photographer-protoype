MEET THE PHOTOGRAPHER

Photographer | Filmn creater

Hi, I'm Jauna, a photographer and filmmaker from New York City. Growing up, I wacthed people, life and the city itself transform around me, inspiring me to capture these fleeting moments. Photography became my way of preserving memories that are as meaningful to me as they are to you. Through my lens, I create images and videos that hold onto life's most special moments, because these aren't just memories, they're my passion.

Capture every moment with vibrant detail and creativity! From intimate portraits to the energy of street scenes, fashions flair and memorable events, I bring a unique vision to each shot. My passion for landscape photography also lets me frame nature's beauty in compelling ways.
